About FlipHawk

Our Mission

FlipHawk was created to democratize arbitrage opportunities for everyone. We believe in giving people the tools to find profitable deals across online marketplaces without needing specialized knowledge or expensive software.

Our goal is to help you make informed buying and selling decisions by identifying price discrepancies in real-time.

How FlipHawk Works

FlipHawk scans online marketplaces like eBay to find items that are being sold at different price points. Our algorithm:

  1. Searches for listings in your selected categories
  2. Uses advanced text matching to identify the same or similar items
  3. Compares prices to find discrepancies
  4. Calculates potential profit and ROI
  5. Assigns confidence scores based on title similarity and other factors

Understanding Confidence Scores

Our confidence score helps you gauge the reliability of an arbitrage opportunity:

90-100%

High confidence - Very likely the same item with a significant price difference

80-89%

Good confidence - Likely the same item with minor differences

70-79%

Moderate confidence - Similar items with potential differences

Below 70%

Low confidence - May be different items or conditions
